# 🛒 Zepto SQL Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset (Zepto) using SQL to extract meaningful business insights related to pricing, discounts, and inventory management. The goal is to understand product performance and support better business decision-making.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **MySQL** – Database management and querying
* **SQL** – Data cleaning, transformation, and analysis

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains product-level information including:

* Category and product name
* Maximum Retail Price (MRP)
* Discount percentage
* Discounted selling price
* Available quantity
* Product weight
* Stock availability (In stock / Out of stock)

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Top Products by Discount

* Identified products offering the highest discounts
* Helps understand pricing strategies and promotions

### 🔹 Category-wise Revenue Estimation

* Calculated estimated revenue using available inventory
* Identifies top-performing categories

### 🔹 Out-of-Stock Analysis

* Analyzed products that are out of stock
* Indicates high demand or supply chain gaps

### 🔹 Price per Gram Evaluation

* Compared product value based on price per gram
* Helps identify best-value products

### 🔹 Product Segmentation

* Classified products into Low, Medium, and Bulk categories based on weight

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

* High-discount products tend to attract more customers
* Certain categories contribute significantly to total revenue
* Some high-value products are frequently out of stock, indicating demand gaps
* Price-per-gram analysis helps identify cost-effective products
